Worked examples

A worked out example or worked example is a good source to learn a new concept. It basically demonstrates the steps required to complete a task inorder to solve a problem.
By scaffolding the learning, worked examples support skill acquisition and reduce the cognitive load while learning a new topic.

For our study , we will be learning about outliers in the form of worked examples. We will have different versions of the worked examples. So you will have the chance to learn about outliers in different ways.
